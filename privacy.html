Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 8, 2026

DAIKY INC ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share personal data when you use the Daiky mobile application and the Daiky website (https://daiky.us) (together, the "Service"). For users in the European Union (EU/EEA), DAIKY INC acts as the Data Controller under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Although we are a U.S.-based company, we comply with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR requirements where relevant. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Service.

2. Data We Collect

A. Information You Provide Voluntarily

Account Data

When you create an account (including via social login), we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Name or username
  • Profile picture (if provided)

Skin & Cosmetic Profile Data

To personalize cosmetic analysis and recommendations, you may voluntarily provide:

  • Skin type (e.g., oily, dry, sensitive)
  • Cosmetic preferences
  • Known cosmetic-related allergies or sensitivities

This information does not constitute medical data. Where required by law, it is processed based on your explicit consent, which can be withdrawn at any time via app settings.

User-Generated Content

You may choose to submit content such as reviews, comments, and images of cosmetic products.

B. Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data

  • Products scanned
  • Features used
  • Interaction history
  • Time spent in the App

Device & Technical Data

  • IP address
  • Device model
  • Operating system and app version
  • Unique device identifiers

Analytics Data

We may collect analytics data to understand how the Service is used and to improve performance and user experience. Where required by law (e.g., EU/EEA), analytics are activated only after your explicit consent.

C. App Permissions

Camera Access

We may request access to your device's camera to enable barcode scanning and other in-app features. Camera access is used only to provide scanning functionality and is not used to record you.

You can grant or withdraw this permission at any time through your device settings. However, disabling camera access may prevent certain features from functioning.

D. Social Logins

You may create an account or log in using third-party sign-in methods (such as social login providers). When you choose this option, we may receive certain information from the provider (for example, your name, email address, and profile picture, depending on your settings). We use this information solely to create and secure your account and to provide the Service.

We do not control how third-party providers process your data and recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies.

3. Subscriptions & Payments

DAIKY offers paid subscriptions through in-app purchases. Payments are processed securely by third-party payment providers. We do not collect or store full payment card details.

Payment-related data (such as transaction identifiers or subscription status) is used only to:

  • Manage subscriptions
  • Enable premium features
  • Handle billing, renewals, and cancellations

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide the Service — Display product scores, ingredient analyses, scan history, and account features.
  • Personalization — Adapt recommendations based on your skin profile and preferences.
  • Service Improvement — Analyze usage trends to improve features, algorithms, and user experience.
  • Security & Fraud Prevention — Protect users, systems, and infrastructure from misuse.
  • Artificial Intelligence & My Daiky — Certain features (including "My Daiky") rely on automated systems or AI. Interactions may be analyzed only in anonymized and aggregated form to improve the Service, generate statistical insights, and enhance algorithmic performance. No decisions with legal or similarly significant effects are made solely through automated processing.
  • Anonymized & Aggregated Analytics — We may anonymize and aggregate data (with all personal identifiers removed) to produce market insights, research, and analytics. Individual users can never be identified from this data.

5. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

We process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a Contract — to provide core Service features
  • Consent — for skin profile data, analytics, and optional features
  • Legitimate Interests — for security, fraud prevention, and anonymized analytics
  • Legal Obligations — where required by law

6. Cookies & Similar Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to ensure basic functionality, improve performance, and understand how the website is used.

Where required by law, we request your consent before placing non-essential cookies. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings and, where applicable, through our consent banner.

7. Data Storage & International Transfers

Personal data may be hosted on servers located in the United States or other jurisdictions.

For transfers from the EU/EEA, we rely on appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, to ensure an adequate level of data protection.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

We may share data only with trusted service providers who help us operate, secure, and improve the Service (such as hosting and infrastructure, analytics and performance monitoring, crash reporting, customer support tools, and subscription or payment management).

These providers are authorized to process data only on our instructions and subject to contractual safeguards.

We may also disclose data if required by law or in connection with a corporate transaction (e.g., merger, acquisition), subject to applicable safeguards.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary:

  • Account data: while your account is active
  • User content: deleted within a reasonable period after account deletion
  • Subscription and transaction records: as required by legal or accounting obligations
  • Anonymized data: may be retained indefinitely for statistical purposes

10. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data.

However, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Use of the Service is at your own risk.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request data portability (where applicable)

Account deletion can be initiated directly in the App settings.

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

DAIKY INC does not sell personal data. You have the right to opt out of any future sale should this practice change.

12. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If such data is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.

13. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the Service. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
